LASER CUTTING
HIGH PRECISION profiles up to 25mm thickness.
Steel Dynamics operates a state of the art Bystronic Laser cutting system offering high precision profiles across a broad spectrum of materials with a profile size up to 6000mm x 2500mm x 25mm thick.
WATERJET CUTTING
HIGH PRECISION profiles up to 150mm thickness.
Waterjet cutting offers a superior edge quality free from the effects of thermal cutting. With more design restrictions and the complexities of material parts being specified
PLASMA CUTTING
PRECISION + COST EFFECTIVE profiles up to 50mm thickness.
Designed to complement both our laser machine and our Large Bed Plasma, this state of the art Hi-Def Plasma cutting system gives a clean precise profile whilst retaining the low cost benefits associated with plasma. High quality, cost effective profiles up to 6000 x 2000 x 50mm thickness.
HD PLASMA CUTTING
HI DEF PLASMA CUTTING
A cost effective cutting method particularly suited to producing a wide variety of production quality standard profiles over a wide thickness range. Steel Dynamics use Submerged & Water Injected plasma which offers best edge quality for this process, and minimised heat input.
